Native Mixed Reality Compositing on Meta Quest 3: A Quantitative Feasibility Study of ARM-Based SoCs and Thermal Headroom

Status: Published Preprint (arXiv)

Authors: Muhammad Kaif Laghari, Areeb Ahmed Shaikh, Faiz Khan, Aafia Gul Siddiqui

This study investigates the feasibility of executing an enhanced MR compositing pipeline natively on the Meta Quest 3 without external PC dependencies. By benchmarking the Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2 against next-generation SoCs, the research demonstrates that current standalone hardware can support 720p30 lightweight compositing at 95% resource utilization, while identifying thermal constraints that limit sustained recording to 5-10 minutes.
